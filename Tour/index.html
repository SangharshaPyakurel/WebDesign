Welcome to Tyamke Travel & Tours


The word "Tyamke" is derived from one of the mountain in Khotang District of Nepal, which is in the eastern part of Nepal. This Mountain is very popular and every year a festival is organized and known by TYAMKE MAHOTSAV and thousands of visitors come here on foot. It has got nice mountainous trails. People can choose this place for mountain hiking and for an adventurous traveling trail.
